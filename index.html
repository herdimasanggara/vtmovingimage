Throughout this semester, these students explored the possibilities of what it means when people say “video” beyond its preconceived conventions. What does “motion graphics” actually mean? Is it similar to “moving images” or “art videos”? Are they interchangeable? Or is the line of demarcation between them more obscure than we initially thought?

This website contains works by students from Moving Image class as taught by Herdimas Anggara at Virginia Tech's School of Visual Arts in Fall 2021. Click the student's name to view their final project. The rotation speed for each bar is relative to respective student's video length.
